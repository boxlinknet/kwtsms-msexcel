Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the kwtSMS for Excel add-in collects, uses, and protects your information.

Data Collection

The kwtSMS for Excel add-in does not collect or share any personal data with third parties. Your data is only transmitted to kwtSMS.com when you explicitly send SMS messages or perform related operations.

Credentials

Your API credentials (username and API key) are stored securely in your Excel document settings. All communication with kwtSMS.com is encrypted using HTTPS to protect your credentials in transit. Your credentials are never shared with any third party.

Spreadsheet Data

Spreadsheet data including phone numbers and message content is sent to kwtSMS.com only when you initiate an SMS send operation. This data is used exclusively to deliver your SMS messages and is processed according to kwtSMS.com's SMS gateway services. Data is not retained longer than necessary for message delivery.

Logging

The add-in maintains local logs of all SMS send operations within your Excel workbook. These logs remain local to your document and are not sent to any external server. Logs include details such as phone numbers, timestamps, and delivery status.

Third-Party Services

The kwtSMS for Excel add-in communicates with kwtSMS.com exclusively for SMS delivery. No other third-party services are used. When you send SMS messages, your phone numbers and message content are transmitted to kwtSMS.com for message delivery. For details on how kwtSMS.com handles data, please refer to their privacy policy.

Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communication with kwtSMS.com
  • Secure storage of credentials in Excel document settings
  • No transmission of unnecessary personal data
  • Local logging with no external data retention

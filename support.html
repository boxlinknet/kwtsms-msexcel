kwtSMS for Excel

Send bulk SMS messages directly from your spreadsheets with powerful mapping, validation, and security features.

Getting Started

Sign up at kwtsms.com
Create your free kwtSMS account at kwtsms.com to access the SMS gateway.
Get API credentials from dashboard
Log in to your kwtSMS dashboard and generate your API credentials. You will need these to authenticate with the add-in.
Open Excel and launch the add-in
Open Microsoft Excel, go to the Insert tab, and launch the kwtSMS for Excel add-in.
Enter credentials and login
Enter your API credentials in the add-in login form and click login to authenticate with kwtSMS.
Map columns and send
Map your spreadsheet columns to phone numbers and message content, configure options, and send your SMS campaign.

Features

Bulk SMS from Spreadsheets
Send SMS messages in bulk directly from your Excel spreadsheets without leaving the application.
Column Mapping
Flexibly map any column in your spreadsheet to phone numbers or message content.
80+ Country Validation
Automatic phone number validation across 80+ countries with localized formatting rules.
Message Cleaning
Automatic message text cleanup and optimization for SMS delivery.
Test Mode
Test your SMS campaigns before sending with full validation without incurring costs.
Logging
Comprehensive logging of all SMS send operations stored locally within your workbook.
